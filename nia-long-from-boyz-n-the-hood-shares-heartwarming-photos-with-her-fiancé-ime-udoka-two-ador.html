Nia Long from Boyz n the Hood Shares Heartwarming Photos with Her Fianc Ime Udoka &amp; Two Ador

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, September 13, 2024

Nia Long's family looked enchanting in new photos she shared on social media. This comes in the wake of her dad's demise.

Hollywood actress, Nia Long took to Instagram to show off her family. The "Single Mom's Club" actress looked ever-beautiful beside her equally stunning family.

The Instagram posts come days following the passing of her dad, Doughty "Doc" Long, who passed on in January.

Nia Long at the LA Premiere of "47 Meters Down Uncaged" on August 13, 2019. | Photo: Getty Images

NIA LONG'S BEAUTIFUL FAMILY

In the Instagram post, it seems the lovely family was out and enjoying a suit-and-tie event as they posed for the camera. Nia's fiance and their sons, all looked dapper in fitting Tuxedos while she dazzled in a dark ensemble. She sweetly captioned the post with a heart emoji:

"My Clique ❤️"

The star actress wasn't done with fans as she shared another post showing an up-close look of her younger son. The little boy looked adorable and could pass for his mommy's twinnie as he flashed a smile. Fans couldn't help gushing over the young boy while they mentioned the striking resemblance between mom and son.

ON HER DAD'S DEATH

Following the patriarch's passing, Nia Long released a soul-stirring statement where she penned a tribute to late "Doc" Long. In her write up, Long described the late poet's achievements, which summed up to black excellence.

Nia and her NBA alum finance, Ime Udoka, have been together for a while.

She wrote: "A poet with stories to tell about the Black experience in America. He wasn't afraid to raise his fist in solidarity for everything Black and proud." While going on to add that he'll be greatly missed, she also took to Instagram, where she shared a photo of him with a large crown emoji placed on his head.

NIA'S ROMANTIC LIFE

Through her long-standing career, Nia Long has never been married, and once during an interview, the actress explained her thoughts about the issue of marriage.

She talked about marriage during a press round in 2013 for "The Best Man Holiday." She made it known that an ambitious and success-driven woman might watch time whirl by and then later realize that she hasn't gotten married or have any kids.

But she isn't fazed by this as, according to her, everyone has a stipulated time. Nia and her NBA alum finance, Ime Udoka, have been together for a while, and they share a son, Kez. Kez is the younger brother to Massai, Long's son, from an older relationship.
