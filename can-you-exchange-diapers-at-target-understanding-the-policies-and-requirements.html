November, 2023 – We all know how challenging it can be to find the perfect size diaper for your little one. This generous timeframe allows parents to ensure that they have the right fit for their bundle of joy without feeling rushed. Notably, this policy extends even if you don’t have a receipt; however, in such cases, instead of receiving a refund, you’ll receive store credit.

Simply bring the incorrectly sized diaper back to the store along with your proof of purchase, and they will happily swap it for the correct size.

It’s worth noting that Target’s exchange policy follows similar terms to its return policy. As long as your item meets the criteria mentioned earlier (within 90 days from purchase date, unopened, and accompanied by proof of purchase), you can replace it with another productregardless of brand or size.

What about exchanges without receipts? While they may typically be challenging at some retailers, Target understands that situations arise where receipts are misplaced or forgotten. If it has been less than 90 days since your diaper purchase and the packaging remains unopened, there is still a reasonably good chance that you can exchange them for a different size.

In conclusion, Target’s return and exchange policies make it easier than ever for parents to achieve the perfect fit when it comes to diapers. With their extended 90-day window, leniency regarding receipts, and commitment to customer satisfaction, Target proves why it is a go-to destination for all your baby care needs.
