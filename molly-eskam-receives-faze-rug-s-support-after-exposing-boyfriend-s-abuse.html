Molly Eskam Receives Faze Rugs Support after Exposing Boyfriends Abuse

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, August 26, 2024

Trigger Warning: The content includes references to topics such as domestic violence. Reach out to the National Domestic Violence Hotline at 1-800-799-7233.

Just hours after Molly Eskam exposed domestic violence from her abusive ex boyfriend, one of her former partners and FaZe Clan member, FaZe Rug, has come out in her support.

On one of her posts revealing her situation during the abuse, Rug commented some words of support for his ex girlfriend.

FaZe Rug's Support for Molly Eskam

Eskam uploaded two videos depicting the domestic abuse she had received at the hands of her ex boyfriend on January 23, 2022.

The first upload was a video, which consisted of slideshows of various images regarding the abuse.

It showed bruises and marks on her body and broken glasses, presumably from the altercations she had with her ex boyfriend.

Written over these slides was, "I can no longer take the abuse I have to be strong I am in so much pain."

Further, the video showed a broken computer case on what looks to be a kitchen floor and blood in various places in her home.

The caption of the post exuded how she felt about posting the videos. She said it was difficult for her to talk about or reveal these things.

The second part of these revelations was a recording of a conversation they had.

In the video, she could be heard crying about her boyfriend being mean and abusive.

The boyfriend, on the other hand, threatened her with more physical violence saying nobody cared that she was crying.

In the second post, she wrote that she stayed with the person because she loved him.

But, she revealed she had left the person that day and encouraged someone else in her position to do the same.

"It never gets better just worse," she concluded the post.

On both these posts, there has been an outpouring of love and support for Eskam. One of these supporters was her ex boyfriend, FaZe Rug.

"Actually disgusting," Rug wrote in the comment section. "You deserve way better."

He talked about how amazing she was and that she was always carrying positive energy.

Finally, he said, "Keep your head up," followed by praying hands and a red heart emoji.

FaZe Rug's supportive comment on Molly Eskam's post about domestic violence. (Photo: Instagram)

Others, too, had similar things to say about her. Many said that she was an incredible person and deserved the world.

People also wished the law enforcement handled this situation sooner rather than later.

Eskam revealed a lot more details about her abusive relationship on her TikTok.

Molly Eskam and FaZe Rug's Relationship

Eskam and Rug were in a relationship for a while in the past. She reportedly gained a following after appearing regularly on his videos.

They got to know each other in 2017 and got closer over the next year and a half. They started dating somewhere around the beginning of 2018, as Rug recalled during his interview on Impaulsive.

Molly Eskam with ex boyfriend and close friend FaZe Rug. (Photo: Twitter)

Eskam, too, said they had a great relationship, even before they got together. They had similar senses of humor and liked each other.

They dated for two months. But Eskam and Rug did not see their relationship going anywhere. Rug also admitted that they didn't want to lose their friendship.

So they decided to break up, and that was it for their relationship.
