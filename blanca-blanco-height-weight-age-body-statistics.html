Blanca Blanco is an American actress known for portraying various important roles in popular films like Fake News, Sensory Perception, Bullet, and Awakened. She has done endorsement work for brands like Lululemon, Lula Mena, Estel, and Chopard. Education

Blanca Blanco received her Associate of Science degree from Spokane Falls Community College in Spokane, Washington. In 2003, she obtained her B.S. degree in psychology from the Washington State University. Thereafter, she studied at the Eastern Washington University and received her Master’s degree in social work.

While in Los Angeles, she also took acting classes from institutes such as Gordon Hunt’s Acting Class in 2008, The Groundlings Theater and School in 2013, and Harry Mastrogeorge Class in 2015.
