Stephen Curry’s Net Worth and Business Ventures

Net Worth and Career Earnings

Stephen Curry, the renowned guard for the Golden State Warriors, has amassed a staggering net worth of $160 million. While his on-court performances have undoubtedly contributed to his wealth, Curry’s financial success extends far beyond the basketball court. In fact, he was ranked as the eighth highest-paid athlete in the world in 2023, earning a remarkable $100 million in a single year, according to Forbes. This impressive figure is a testament to Curry’s exceptional skills and marketability.

Throughout his career, Curry has secured several lucrative contracts that have significantly bolstered his earnings. In 2017, he made history by signing the NBA’s first-ever $200 million contract with the Golden State Warriors. This groundbreaking deal solidified his status as one of the highest-paid players in the league. Not content with resting on his laurels, Curry continued to negotiate remarkable contracts, including a remarkable $215 million four-year contract extension in 2021. With such substantial earnings, it is no wonder that Curry’s financial success has reached unprecedented heights.

Business Ventures and Partnerships

Beyond his remarkable achievements on the basketball court, Stephen Curry has proven himself to be a shrewd entrepreneur and investor. He has successfully diversified his portfolio by establishing various businesses and partnerships that have further enhanced his wealth. One of his notable collaborations is with sportswear giant Under Armour. Since 2013, Curry has been associated with the brand, and together they have created the highly successful Curry Brand, which encompasses a range of sneakers and athletic wear. In recognition of his contributions, Curry was awarded $75 million worth of shares in Under Armour in April 2023 for assuming the role of president of Curry Brand and remaining an ambassador for the company.

In addition to his partnership with Under Armour, Curry has founded his own production company, Unanimous Media. This venture focuses on developing, producing, and distributing various film and TV projects, as well as podcasts. Notable works include the feature documentary “Underrated,” which chronicles Curry’s basketball journey, and “Jumpshot,” a documentary that pays homage to Kenny Sailors, the inventor of the jump shot in basketball. Unanimous Media has forged partnerships with industry giants such as Comcast NBCUniversal and Audible, further solidifying Curry’s presence in the entertainment industry.

Furthermore, Curry has demonstrated his entrepreneurial spirit by launching his own line of bourbon, Gentleman’s Cut Kentucky Straight Bourbon Whiskey. This premium brand has quickly gained popularity, with each bottle retailing for approximately $73. In addition to whiskey, the brand also offers a range of tumblers and sweatshirts, catering to the diverse interests of Curry’s fan base.

It is worth noting that Curry’s business ventures extend beyond the realms of sports and entertainment. He has also invested in Tonal, a smart home gym startup valued at around $600 million as of 2023. This investment showcases Curry’s keen eye for innovative and promising ventures outside of his primary field of expertise.

In conclusion, Stephen Curry’s net worth and business ventures exemplify his remarkable success both on and off the basketball court.
