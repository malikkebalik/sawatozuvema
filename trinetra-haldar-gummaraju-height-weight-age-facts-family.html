Trinetra Haldar Gummaraju Facts

  • She was raised in Bangalore, Karnataka in a Telugu/Bengali-speaking well-to-do family.
  • Haldar spent the first twenty years of her life as a male during which time, she experienced gender dysphoria. It was in her teenage years that she began to embrace her female side and changed her name to “Trinetra.” After that, she went through her sex reassignment surgery and her family relocated to Bengaluru.
  • Her family was supportive of her decision to change her gender.
  • She was listed on Forbes‘ “30 Under 30 – Asia – Media, Marketing & Advertising” list in 2022.
  • Trinetra made her debut as an actress in 2023 as Meher Chaudhry in Prime Video‘s Made in Heaven.
  • She is a prominent LGBTQIA+ supporter.
  • Trinetra has a pet Golden Retriever named Bruno.
  • She is a non-vegetarian.
  • Haldar is the first-ever doctor who is transgender.
  • In her free time, she enjoys doodling and drawing.
