Erin Krakow is a respected American actress who commenced her career in 2010 with her television debut on the Lifetime drama, Army Wives. She is best known for her role as Elizabeth Thatcher Thornton in the 2014 film When Calls the Heart.

Biography and Profile Summary

Erin Krakow was born on September 5, 1984, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, to Dr. Elliott and Morgan Krakow. She was raised in Wellington, Florida, alongside her younger brother, Kyle. Erin, who is of white ethnicity, holds American nationality.

She attended the Alexander W. Dreyfoos Jr. School of the Arts for her acting studies and later earned a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree from New York City’s Juilliard School.

Net Worth

Age, Height and Weight

Erin was born on 5 September 1984 and stands at 5 feet 9 inches (1.75 m) tall, weighing 61 kg (134 lbs.).

Career

Erin embarked on her acting journey in high school, appearing in productions such as George the Fourth and The Importance of Being Earnest. Her television debut was in 2010 as Tanya Gabriel in Army Wives, and she later appeared in Chance at Romance and as the lead in When Calls the Heart.
