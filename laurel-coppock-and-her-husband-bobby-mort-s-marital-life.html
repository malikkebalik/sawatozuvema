Laurel Coppock And Her Husband, Bobby Mort's Marital Life.

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

Famous American actress and broadway dancer, Laurel Coppock is widely recognized for her appearance in the comedy movies, Crazy, Stupid, Love, Modern Family, 2 Broke Girls and many more. Also, she is known as the founding member of The Groundlings. Her nickname is Jan the Toyota Lady.

Coppock was born on 22nd August 1970, in the United States of America. Born to her American parents, she is of White ethnicity and holds American nationality.

Marital Status: Married

Laurel is a married woman; she tied the knot with her partner Bobby Mort. However, none of them mentioned their wedding date and venue. 

Jan the Toyota Lady, and her spouse share a daughter from their nuptial, whose name is yet to be disclosed. Back in 2014, Coppock's pregnancy was revealed via advertisement of Toyota car.

In 2018, Laurel's fans discovered a second baby bump on Coppock. Similarly, on 28th March 2018, a fan of Laurel named NAIL tweeted, saying ''Is @Toyota #toyotajan pregnant again or just old commercials?'' 

The Toyota USA replied, saying, Yes, Jan is pregnant. However, Coppock has not discussed her second child.

Before Fame

Coppock developed her interest in acting since her early days. She completed her high school via Colby College based in Maine and joined the Square Acting Conservatory. 

During the time, Coppock was studying and performing at the same time. She used to perform at Chicago's Second City and The iO Theater. 

A few months later, Laurel went to Amsterdam and performed with Boom Chicago theater group. Her career pursuit is quite similar to another actor Adrian Lester who also started his career as a theater artist.

Acting Career

In 2008, the theater actress made her debut appearance in the movie Food Chain. She gained immense popularity which led her to portray in various movies such as Crazy, Stupid, Love, along with Steve Carell and Ryan Gosling.

Coppock is also widely known for her appearance in the advertisement of Toyota USA. In the ad, she appears as Jan, who is an employee of Toyota.

Some of Laurel's recent work includes her voice as Amy Morgan and Cathy Walker in the Television series Mike Tyson Mysteries.

Net Worth

Laurel Coppock makes decent money from her career as an actress and voice artist. To date, she has portrayed in numerous movies, television series and contributed her voice, which adds up a decent fortune to her current wealth.

As per some sources, Laurel receives paycheque more than $150,000 for a single episode on any series. Additionally, Coppock is the co-founder of Grounding and one of the most popular Toyota's advertisement girl as Jan.

One of her famous portrayal movie, Crazy, Stupid, Love grossed $142.8 million worldwide. Laurel's total net worth is estimated to be more than $1 million as of 2019, which is more than another actress Hannah Locher with a prosperity of $500,000.
