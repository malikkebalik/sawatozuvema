The former actor, Andrew Shue, is known for his role as Bolly Campbell in the Television Series, Melrose Place from 1992 to 1999. As of now, Andrew co-hosts a webisodes series, Mad Life, along with Chuck Nice. He is also a co-founder of a social networking site, CafeMom.

Andrew Shue was born Andrew Eppley Shue on February 20, 1967, in Wilmington, Delaware. He is among the two children born to James W. Shue and his wife, Anne Brewster. His father worked as a lawyer, and his mother was a bank executive working as vice president. His sister, Elisabeth, is also an actress. 

Is Andrew Shue Married, or is Single?

Andrew Shue is apparently single currently. He was previously married to Amy Robach. The former couple finalized their divorce on March 17, 2023. They stayed married for a dozen years.

The extramarital affair between Amy and her co-worker T.J Holmes made their marital life to be doomed. During their divorce, the agreement for the custody of their shared dog, Brody was also difficult to solve.

Amy is a Television Presenter for ABC News. The love birds walked down the aisle on February 6, 2010. The lavish wedding ceremony was held at The Lighthouse at Chelsea Piers, adjacent to the Hudson River. The couple dated each other for some time before tying the knot. 

Although they were married for 12 years, they did not welcome any children. Andrew first met Amy at a Book Party in April 2009. After dating for some time, they got engaged in September 2009.

Relationship History of Andrew

Many people believe that this is the only marriage of Andrew. However, Andrew was married and divorced once before tying the knot with Amy. Andrew was married to Jennifer Hageney in 1994. 

The duo welcomed their first son, Nathaniel, in 1996. Then in 1999, they had their second son, Aiden. Andrew and Jennifer were blessed with their third son, Wyatt, in 2004. The actor divorced his wife in 2008. 

Andrew's Ex-Wife Amy Shares A similar Relationship History 

Amy was previously married and divorced before tying her wedding knot with Andrew. She was married to Tim McIntosh who is a former New Zealand cricket player. They tied the knot in 1996. 

The former couple, Amy and Tim were blessed with two daughters, Ava, born in 2002, and Analise in 2006. For an undisclosed reason, the couple filed for a joint divorce in 2008 and moved apart. Soon after the divorce, Amy tied the knot with Andrew.
