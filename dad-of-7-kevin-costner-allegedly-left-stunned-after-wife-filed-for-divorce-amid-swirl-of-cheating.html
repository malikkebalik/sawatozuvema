Dad-Of-7 Kevin Costner Allegedly Left Stunned after Wife Filed for Divorce amid Swirl of 'Cheating'

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, August 12, 2024
  • Kevin Costner's friend revealed the actor's reaction to his divorce and commented on whether he cheated.
  • Details from their upcoming divorce have been revealed.

68-year-old Kevin Costner is an American actor. He is also a producer, director, and musician who is most famous for his roles in titles like "Field of Dreams," "The Untouchables," and "The Bodyguard."

He was happily married for some years. However, that union has recently begun to fall apart. The pair have decided to split. The details of the divorce have recently been shared with the public.

Kevin Costner and his wife, Christine at a New York Yankees versus Tampa Bay Devil Rays game at Yankee Stadium in the Bronx, NY | Source: Getty Images

Earlier this week, it was revealed that Costner's wife had filed for divorce. They were married for 18 years, and a rep or Costner asked that fans give the family privacy during the tumultuous time.

Costner's wife, Christine, cited "irreconcilable differences" as the reason for the divorce, but Christine has not mentioned what those differences may have been.

Costner's Friend Reveals the Actor's Reaction and Commented on Whether He Cheated

There were rumors that Costner's wife had decided to dissolve their marriage because Costner had an affair. However, a friend of the actor has now confirmed that the tale is inaccurate.

Kevin Costner on the set of 'Molly's Game' in Central Park on January 31, 2017 | Source: Getty Images

Costner's first marriage, which also ended in divorce, was said to be because of infidelity on Costner's part, too. Yet his friend confirmed:

"There was no issue of cheating at all."

The friend also mentioned that Costner was surprised by the divorce and did not want it to happen because he greatly loved his wife and children. Costner and his wife share three children; Cayden, 15; Hayes, 14; and Grace, 12.

Kevin Costner walking in the outfield before the Chicago White Sox and New York Yankees game in 2021 | Source: Getty Images

The couple's last public appearance was at the Oscars in March, and they appeared to be happily married then. His friend mentioned that he was disappointed by Christine filing for divorce.

Custody, Prenup, Details of the Divorce

When Costner's friend was asked if they knew the reason his friend's wife had filed for divorce, the friend mused:

"I have no idea if [Costner] even knows what the reasons are."

Kevin Costner, his wife, Christine, and children Grace Avery Costner, Hayes Logan Costner and Cayden Wyatt Costner arrive at the World Premiere of Disney's "McFarland, USA" at the El Capitan Theatre | Source: Getty Images

They then mentioned that when the "Yellowstone" cast discovered the divorce, they were shocked as Costner did not have to stay on set while filming and often went home to visit his family.

After Costner's wife filed for divorce, she asked that the couple share joint custody of their three children. However, she has not requested spousal support because there is an alleged prenup to protect Costner's $250 million fortune.

Costner's rep declined to talk about anything to do with what the couple had decided to do in terms of their finances. It said circumstances beyond Costner's control had resulted in the dissolution of his marriage.

There is also alleged "separate property" in their agreement that states jewelry, miscellaneous items, and earnings acquired before, during their marriage, and after their separation will remain in possession of the person who originally obtained them.
