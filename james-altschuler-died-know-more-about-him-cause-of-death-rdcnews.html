Who was James Altschuler?

James Altschuler, born on April 22, 1987, hailed from Fresno, California, but later called San Diego home. He dedicated himself to academic pursuits, focusing on Business Economics at UC Santa Barbara. His professional journey took a significant turn when he joined Procore Technologies as an Account Manager. Recognizing his skills and dedication, James swiftly climbed the professional ladder, earning a promotion to the position of Account Executive. In this role, he showcased his expertise by providing thoughtful solutions to new Procore clients.

James’s success didn’t stop there; he continued to rise within the company, eventually taking on the responsibility of Senior Account Executive in the Greater San Diego Area, a role he admirably held for an impressive five years. His commitment and accomplishments made a lasting impact on those who had the privilege of working with him.
