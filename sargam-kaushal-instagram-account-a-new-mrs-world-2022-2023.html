Sargam Koushal, an Indian competitor, was crowned Mrs. World on Sunday at a gala ceremony in Las Vegas. "After 21-22 years, we've reclaimed the throne. I'm overjoyed. "I adore you, India, and the rest of the globe," declared the freshly elected Mrs. World.
