Vinícius Júnior’s Bio

Vinícius José Paixão de Oliveira Júnior, popularly known as Vinícius Júnior or Vini Jr is a Brazilian young sensation making his name shine in the footballing world was born on July 12, 2000, in São Gonçalo, Brazil. Vinicius comes from a very humble family which includes his parents Vinícius José Paixão de Oliveira and Fernanda Oliveira, and two brothers and a sister. He has not only dominated on the domestic level but also had the pleasure of representing Brazil’s National team in the most popular international event FIFA World Cup in 2022-23.

After two prosperous years with Flamengo, Vinícius made a major move to Real Madrid, a top La Liga team, in 2018. Real Madrid signed Vinicius to a $48.76 million contract. Vini’s contract with Real Madrid broke the previous record for the nation’s U-18 player. Since his debut at Real Madrid senior team, he has scored 62 goals in 235 appearances and established himself as a key player for Real Madrid. 

He has made crucial efforts, helping Real Madrid to win the La Liga title in season 2021-22. He continues to impress viewers and scored a match-winning goal in the UEFA Champions League final of 2022. His journey is a perfect example of how sport can change the lives of people, from playing in the streets of Rio to representing his national side and making a record-breaking signing with the most successful club in football.

Vinícius Júnior’s Career

Vinícius Júnior started his footballing journey by playing Futsal at local academies before joining Flamengo at a young age. After copping up with early adaptability problems he continued to show his skills on the training grounds and made his Flamengo senior debut in 2017. He played for Flamengo with strong determination and extreme talent for two lively years. 

His talent and speed were recognized by Real Madrid in the year 2017 which made a deal to sign him for the club but he played his debut game in September 2018, coming in as a substitute in the 87th minute. Through this transfer, Vinicius made history by becoming the highest-paid athlete by the age of 18 years in Brazil. He currently is a teammate of Jude Bellingham in Real Madrid.

Vinicius represent Brazil in 2022 FIFA world cup and 2021 Copa América on international stage. Being a well-known football player in Brazil, Vinicius also manages his platform Vini Jr. to actively oppose racism in sports. This institute also runs an app named Base with the motive of educating and inspiring young kids in Brazil.

His firm determination and hard work have earned him a lot of success right from the start of his career, winning the South American U-17 Championship Best Player 2017 and UEFA Champions League Young Player of the Season 2021-22 awards. He is the only Brazilian to win the Golden Ball award in FIFA 2022 and adds his name to the list with the likes of football legends Cristiano Ronaldo, Lionel Messi, and Luka Modric.
