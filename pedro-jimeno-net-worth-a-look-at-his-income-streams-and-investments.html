Pedro Jimeno’s Biography

Pedro Jimeno is a well-known Dominican-American reality TV star, rapper, and businessman. He’s famous for being on TV shows like 90 Day Fiancé and The Family Chantel. Pedro was born on October 3, 1991, in the Dominican Republic, in a small place called Mao, Valverde.

He grew up with his mom, Lidia Jimeno, and his younger sister, Nicole Jimeno. His dad left when he was young and started another family. He went to school at the Technological Institute of The Americas in the Dominican Republic and worked as a Spanish tutor. That’s how he met Chantel Everett when she came to visit.

They fell in love, and in 2016, Pedro applied for a K-1 visa to move to the United States and marry Chantel. Their journey was shown on TV in the fourth season of 90 Day Fiancé, and they got married in June 2017.

Pedro and Chantel have been on more TV shows, like The Family Chantel and Pillow Talk, but their relationship has had its ups and downs.

Besides his reality TV career, Pedro is a rapper. He’s released songs like “No Te Compares” and “La Chapa” and even went on tour with other reality TV stars. Pedro is also a businessman. He owns a real estate company in Atlanta, Georgia, and has his own clothing line.
