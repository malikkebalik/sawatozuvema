Ryujin is a South Korean singer, rapper, dancer, actress, and TV personality who gained popularity as she debuted with the girl group, ITZY, on February 12, 2019. ITZY also includes Lia, Hwang Ye-ji, Chaeryeong, and Yuna and they have all worked together on a number of musical projects which include several singles, EPs, and albums like It’z Icy, It’z Summer, Cherry, Dalla Dalla, It’z Different, and Icy. Ryujin was discovered and scouted by JYPE while she was attending a GOT7 fan meet and later went on to work on several shows and movies, such as Knowing Bros, Mix Nine, Stray Kids, Idol Room, and The King, as well. As a member of ITZY, she has become a popular artist worldwide while ITZY enjoys a huge social media fan base with more than 3 million followers on Instagram, more than a million followers on Twitter, more than 800k followers on Facebook, and more than 2 million subscribers on YouTube.

Best Known For

  • Being the main rapper, lead dancer, vocalist, and center of the South Korean girl group named ITZY which also consists of Lia, Hwang Ye-ji, Chaeryeong, and Yuna and has released several tracks, EPs, and albums as well
  • Having released the EP, titled It’z Icy, as a part of ITZY, on July 29, 2019, via JYP Entertainment. It comprises of songs such as Icy, It’z Summer, Cherry, Want It? Ryujin Facts

  • She was attending a fan meet for the popular South Korean boy band, GOT7, when she was scouted by JYP Entertainment.
  • ITZY‘s debut was announced by JYP Entertainment on January 21, 2019, and a video trailer was also released on JYPE’s channel which revealed the group members.
  • In 2017, Ryujin was cast as a contestant on the JTBC survival reality show, Mix Nine. She performed really well on the show and was ranked at number #1 amongst all the girls. However, she was unable to make her debut as the girls’ team lost to the boys’ team.

  • She enjoys food that tastes spicy as well as savory.
  • Her acting credits also include having played a part in the movie titled The King in 2017.
  • Ryujin was also cast in the Stray Kids‘ Survival Show in 2017.
  • She was cast as a guest in an episode of the game talk-show series, Idol Room, in July 2019.
  • She has often said that her role model has been the famous singer, record producer, activist, actress, and television presenter, Lee Hyori.
  • Interestingly enough, the CEO of YG Entertainment also once offered Ryujin to join the entertainment company. However, she decided to stick with JYP Entertainment only.
  • She had trained with JYP Entertainment for around 4 years before she debuted with ITZY on February 12, 2019.
  • Ryujin enjoys watching movies and taking pictures during her leisure time.
  • As a member of ITZY, she has won several awards and nominations which include the likes of Genie Music Award, Soribada Best K-Music Award, Brand of the Year Award, and several others.
  • She has also been roommates with her bandmate, Yeji.
  • Ryujin shares a friendly bond with Dreamcatcher‘s Jiu and ELRIS‘ Bella as well as with LOONA‘s Heejin and Hyunjin.
  • She pets 2 cats named Byun and Dal.
  • In March 2023, Ryujin became a member of The Promise’s Honors Club, which recognizes donors who have donated over 100 million won to The Promise Foundation.
