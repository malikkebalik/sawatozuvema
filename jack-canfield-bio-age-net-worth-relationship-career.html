Jack Canfield | Bio, Age, Net Worth, Relationship, Career

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 10, 2024

American author, businessman, motivational speaker, and corporate trainer Jack Canfield works in these fields.

He is most known for co-authoring the Chicken Soup for the Soul book series, which has over 250 titles and has been published in more than 40 languages to the tune of 500 million copies.

Jack taught at a high school in the inner city before becoming a best-selling novelist.

Where was Jack Canfield born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, and Education

Jack Canfield was born on August 19, 1944, in Fort Worth, Texas, in the United States of America. He has not disclosed his family members.

He graduated from Harvard University with a BA in Chinese history in 1962, and the University of Massachusetts awarded him a Master’s degree in Chinese history in 1973.

Is Jack Canfield Married? Relationship

Before he married Inga, his present wife, he was previously married twice. Jack’s first wife, Judith Ohlbaum, was his wife from 1971 to 1976. Similarly, from September 1978 to December 1999, he married Georgia Lee Noble, who served as his second wife.

Jack has five children from his three marriages: Riley Jane Mahoney, Travis Mahoney, Christopher Noble Canfield, and Oran David Canfield, a drummer in a band, artist, and bicycle messenger. Kyle Dania Canfield is a hip-hop singer and songwriter.
