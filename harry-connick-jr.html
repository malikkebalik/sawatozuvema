Harry Connick Jr.

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, July 19, 2024
On 11-9-1967 Harry Connick Jr. was born in New Orleans, Louisiana. He made his 28 million dollar fortune with When My Heart Finds Christmas, Only You & Hope Floats. The musician is married to Jill Goodacre, his starsign is Virgo and he is now 56 years of age.
Harry Connick Jr. is an American musician with over 28 million albums sold worldwide. He's been named one of the top 60 best-selling male artists in the USA and he has had 7 top 20 US albums.

In 1994, he married a former Victoria's Secret model, Jill Goodacre. They have 3 daughters together.
He is also a big supporter of hometown NFL franchise New Orleans Saints.
