Who is Laura Slade Wiggins?

Laura Slade Wiggins is an American actress, musician, and singer who is best known for her role in ‘Shameless’, ‘The Tomorrow People’, ‘Rings’, and ‘Stand’.

Laura Slade Wiggins: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity

She was born on August 8, 1988, in Athens, Georgia, USA. She is currently 30 years old. Her mother’s name is Kathy Wiggins and her father’s name is Mark Wiggins(prominent Athens attorney). She has got a brother named Motie Wiggins.

Her hobbies include Philately and creative writing. She has got a tattoo of a dove underneath her left armpit. She has a dog named Papillio in her home.

Laura holds American citizenship but information about her ethnicity is unknown.

In which school did Laura Slade Wiggins attend?

She joined Pasadena City College, Pasadena, California. She used to dance 15-20 hours per week during high school.

Laura Slade Wiggins: Professional Life and Career

She debuted her career in the TV series ‘Not Like Everyone Else’ in 2008 alongside Lucas Till, Illeana Douglas, J.D. Evermore, Alia Shawkat, and Eric Schweig.

She started appearing in the film in 2008  after she appeared in ‘Dance of the Dead’ directed by Greg Bishop. After that, she made her appearance in several films and TV series like ‘Girl’, ‘Eleventh Hour’, ‘Hit List’, ‘Take Flight’, etc.

She gained more fame after she appeared as Karen Jackson in the series ‘Shameless’ from 2011 to 2013. She portrayed the recurring role in the series ‘The Tomorrow People’ in 2013 and appeared as Carly Rydell in the ‘Law & Order: Special Victims Unit in 2014.

Her recent appearance includes Juliana in the episode ‘Remember the Devil’ in ‘Chicago P.D.’ in 2017, and as Helen Corning in the film ‘Nancy Drew and the Hidden Staircase’ in 2019.

The beautiful actress has not been awarded or nominated for her work to date.
