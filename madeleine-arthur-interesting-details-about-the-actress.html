Professional Journey

In 2011, Madeleine Arthur entered the acting industry with the television series R.L. Stine’s The Haunting Hour where she portrayed the character Meg in a single episode named Dreamcatcher. Following a two-year hiatus, she resumed her acting career with appearances in small roles in The Killing and several other programs.

Her career flourished when she took on the role of Young Willa Warren in the television series The Family, bringing her recognition in the television industry. She has also ventured into films, making her debut as Sally in the horror film Grace: The Possession in 2014. Subsequently, she appeared in other films like Big Eyes (2014), Echo and Soloman (2017), and Colour Out of Space (2019).
