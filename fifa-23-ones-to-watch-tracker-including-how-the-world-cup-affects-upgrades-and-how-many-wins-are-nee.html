FIFA 23 Ones to Watch tracker including how the World Cup affects upgrades and how many wins are nee

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, July 11, 2024

FIFA 23 Ones to Watch player items are now available in Ultimate Team, with a host of new Premier League talent on show.

Manchester City's Erling Haaland naturally become the first player to see his rating increase following his stunning start to the season.

Yet the likes of Arsenal ace Gabriel Jesus, Liverpool star Darwin Nunez and Tottenham's Richarlison are all hoping to follow suit by upgrading.

If you couldn't guess by now, OTW players are dynamic cards that celebrate players who were transferred in the summer transfer window.

Ones To Watch player items will be boosted when the player himself, Haaland for example, receives a Team of the Week or Man of the Match based item in Ultimate Team.

Starting September 30, they are also eligible for a one-time upgrade if their team wins three out of their next eight domestic league games.

In addition, they will receive a further plus-one overall if their national team wins any games at the World Cup in Qatar later this year.

The actual OTW players themselves do not need to feature in the games for club or country for them to count towards the upgrade
