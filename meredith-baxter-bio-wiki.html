Meredith Baxter Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, September 2, 2024

Who is Meredith Baxter?

American Meredith Baxter is an Actress and Producer. Baxter is Debbie Sloan of All the President’s Men and Nancy Lawrence Maitland of Family.

Next, she will be appearing as Dr. Alix Martel in Genesis: The Future of Mankind Is Woman.

What are the birth facts of Meredith Baxter?

Meredith Baxter was born Meredith Ann Baxter on the 21st of June 1947 in South Pasadena, California. She is of British-Irish ancestry.

She has two brothers, Richard, and Brian. They were raised in Pasadena.

Family Background

Her father, Whitney Blake is a director. Also, her mother, Tom Baxter is a radio announcer. However, they parted ways in 1953.

After the divorce, she was raised by her mother along with her siblings. Later, Tom married the comedy writer, Allan Mannings.

Meredith’s Education

Initially, she attended James Monroe High School in Los Angeles. Later, she was transferred to Hollywood High School. From High School, she graduated in 1965.

During her senior year in high school, she also attended Interlochen Center for the Arts.

Starting Journey as Actress

Despite belonging to a film background, she started her career as an actress on her own. She debuted on TV with the series The Interns. However, she was featured in a single episode of the series.

Following that, she appeared in numerous shows like The Young Lawyers, Insight, and Owen Marshall, Counselor at Law in some minor roles for a single episode only.

Producer

Is Baxter recovered from Breast Cancer?

In 1999, it was diagnosed that she has breast cancer. Later, she went for treatment for that. Currently, she is recovering from cancer.

During the treatment, she lost her breast. However, her husband was against the decision. Also, he didn’t show any sort of support for that.
