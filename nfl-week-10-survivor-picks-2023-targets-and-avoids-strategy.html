NFL Week 10 Survivor Picks 2023: Targets and Avoids Strategy

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, July 27, 2024

As the excitement of the NFL season escalates, fans and fantasy leagues alike are on the edge of their seats for the NFL Week 10 Survivor Picks. With the playoff landscape still resembling a tough-to-crack code, Week 10 throws another thrilling puzzle our way. As teams jostle for position and every play carries the weight of potential postseason glory, making the right survivor pick becomes more crucial than ever.

This week is about adaptability, insightful choices, and those critical decisions that could make or break your survivor pool. Buckle up as we delve into the smartest targets and the traps to avoid in Week 10.

NFL Week 10 Survivor Picks 2023: An In-depth Look

Carolina Panthers vs Chicago Bears

  • Spread: Bears (-3); Panthers (+3)
  • Moneyline: Bears (-148); Panthers (+124)
  • Over/under: 40.5
    The Chicago Bears are slight favorites at home with a -3 spread, indicating a close contest against the Panthers. Betting the over/under might be tricky with a low 40.5 points, reflecting expectations of a defensive battle.

Indianapolis Colts vs New England Patriots

  • Spread: Colts (-2); Patriots (+2)
  • Moneyline: Colts (-130); Patriots (+110)
  • Over/under: 43.5
    The Colts are modestly favored against the Patriots on neutral ground in Germany, with only a 2-point advantage. The over/under suggests a moderately scoring game is anticipated by oddsmakers.

Cleveland Browns vs Baltimore Ravens

  • Spread: Ravens (-5); Browns (+5)
  • Moneyline: Ravens (-218); Browns (+180)
  • Over/under: 40.5
    The Ravens are expected to control the game as 5-point favorites against the Browns. The total points are set at a modest 40.5, pointing towards a game where defenses may shine.

Houston Texans vs Cincinnati Bengals

  • Spread: Bengals (-7); Texans (+7)
  • Moneyline: Bengals (-355); Texans (+280)
  • Over/under: 45.5
    The Bengals are heavily favored with a -7 spread against the Texans, suggesting confidence in their ability to win by more than a touchdown. A higher over/under of 45.5 indicates an expectation of offensive firepower.

San Francisco 49ers vs Jacksonville Jaguars

  • Spread: 49ers (-2.5); Jaguars (+2.5)
  • Moneyline: 49ers (-135); Jaguars (+114)
  • Over/under: 44.5
    The 49ers are slightly favored on the road, with the Jaguars given a small +2.5-point cushion. An over/under of 44.5 leans towards a game with some scoring but not a shootout.

New Orleans Saints vs Minnesota Vikings

  • Spread: Saints (-3); Vikings (+3)
  • Moneyline: Saints (-155); Vikings (+130)
  • Over/under: 41
    The Saints, as 3-point favorites, are expected to edge out the Vikings in what oddsmakers see as a relatively low-scoring affair, given the 41-point over/under.

Green Bay Packers vs Pittsburgh Steelers

  • Spread: Steelers (-3); Packers(+3)
  • Moneyline: Steelers (-162); Packers (+136)
  • Over/under: 38
    The Steelers are favored by 3 points over the Packers in what is projected to be one of the week’s lower-scoring games, with an over/under set at 38.

Tennessee Titans vs Tampa Bay Buccaneers

  • Spread: Buccaneers (-1.5); Titans (+1.5)
  • Moneyline: Buccaneers (-125); Titans (+105)
  • Over/under: 39.5
    This matchup is expected to be tight, with the Buccaneers slightly favored. The over/under suggests a defensive game is in the cards, with fewer than 40 points expected.

Atlanta Falcons vs Arizona Cardinals

  • Spread: Falcons (-2.5); Cardinals (+2.5)
  • Moneyline: Falcons (-130); Cardinals (+110)
  • Over/under: 42
    The Falcons are favored on the road, but only by 2.5 points, which hints at a competitive game. The 42-point over/under indicates a moderate-scoring game.

Detroit Lions vs Los Angeles Chargers

  • Spread: Lions (-1); Chargers (+1)
  • Moneyline: Lions (-112); Chargers (-108)
  • Over/under: 48.5
    A near pick’em game with the Lions slightly favored by 1 point. A high over/under of 48.5 suggests expectations for a high-scoring contest.

New York Giants vs Dallas Cowboys

  • Spread: Cowboys (-15); Giants (+15)
  • Moneyline: Cowboys (-1000); Giants (+650)
  • Over/under: 40
    The Cowboys are heavy favorites with a large 15-point spread, suggesting a lopsided game against the Giants. However, the low over/under of 40 points indicates fewer total points being scored.

Washington Commanders vs Seattle Seahawks

  • Spread: Seahawks (-6); Commanders (+6)
  • Moneyline: Seahawks (-245); Commanders (+200)
  • Over/under: 44.5
    The Seahawks, as 6-point favorites, are expected to win comfortably at home against the Commanders. The over/under at 44.5 indicates the potential for a decent scoring game.

New York Jets vs Las Vegas Raiders

  • Over/under: 36.5

Denver Broncos vs Buffalo Bills

  • Spread: Bills (-7.5); Broncos (+7.5)
  • Moneyline: Bills (-380); Broncos (+300)
  • Over/under: 46
