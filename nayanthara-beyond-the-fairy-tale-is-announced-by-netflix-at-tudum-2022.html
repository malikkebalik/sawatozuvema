Nayanthara: Beyond The Fairy Tale Is Announced By Netflix At TUDUM 2022

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, September 25, 2024

The new film is about the life of a lovely couple. It is basically a “intimate documentary” movie titled as Nayanthara: Beyond The Fairytale. Gautham Vasudev Menon will direct the film, which will be produced by Vignesh and Nayanthara’s Rowdy Pictures.

Nayanthara Release Date

They didn’t reveal yet, but hopefully soon.

About Nayanthara’s Upcoming Project

Nayanthara: Beyond The Fairytale will be based on Nayanthara’s real-life wedding to filmmaker Vignesh Sivan. Aside from their wedding, the project will provide an inside look into their lives and beyond. “We try to unpack who the intensely private Nayanthara is, and how Vignesh has made her harness the full power of her talent and love – as an artist and a woman,” according to the official logline of Nayanthara: Beyond The Fairytale.

Nayanthara got married to filmmaker Vignesh Sivan on June 9, 2022.

Cast Of The Documentary Film Nayanthara

Nayanthara and Vignesh Shivan’s dream wedding will soon be the subject of a “wonderful documentary” on Netflix. The film, Nayanthara: Beyond The Fairytale, stars the actor and director as they discuss their high-profile marriages, which were attended by Rajinikanth, Shah Rukh Khan, Suriya, and other celebrities.

Although the release date for Vignesh and Nayanthara’s documentary has yet to be announced, a Netflix advertisement gave viewers a sneak peek into what to expect as the couple discussed their romance, with Vignesh praising Nayanthara as a beautiful soul.

Shah Rukh and several other celebrities attended the wedding, including director Mani Ratnam. Stars such as Katrina Kaif and Samantha Ruth Prabhu congratulated her on social media. The couple exchanged vows in front of their loved ones and family at the Sheraton Grand in Mahabalipuram.

When Netflix Sent Legal Notice To Nayanthara And Vignesh

It was rumored that the OTT giant Netflix was upset with the director for posting Nayanthara and Vignesh’s picture on social media as they have bought all the rights to the wedding video.

It was also reported that Netflix sent the couple a legal notice demanding that they return the price of wedding rights paid to the couple, which was estimated to be around 25 crores.

At the moment, it appears that everything between Netflix and the actor-filmmaker couple has been settled.
