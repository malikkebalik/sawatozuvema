Models, Mass Effect: Andromeda Points of interest

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

This is a list of all ship models that can be found in Mass Effect: Andromeda. They are displayed above the desk in Pathfinder's quarters on the Tempest.

Missable models!

Initiative Fighter Model - Spender's Apartment on Nexus, you can pick it up while visiting this room during Nakmor Drack: Krogan Betrayal mission. If model is not collected before leaving the apartment, it'll no longer be retrievable.

Mission rewards

Tempest Model - you will receive it at the end of Movie Night: The Final Piece mission.

Some models you can buy in stores / from merchants

N01 Nomad Model - General Merchant, Nexus / Hyperion

Nexus Model - General Merchant, Nexus / Hyperion

Archon's Flagship Model - General Merchant, Nexus / Hyperion (after you complete Hunting the Archon)

Citadel Model - General Merchant, Nexus / Hyperion (after you complete The Journey to Meridian)

Kett Shuttle Model - Thrasia, Kadara

Remnant Fighter Model - Generel Goods Merchant, Aya

More

Remnant Derelict Model - On the table in Alec's HQ during or after completing Epilogue: Home and Away mission.
