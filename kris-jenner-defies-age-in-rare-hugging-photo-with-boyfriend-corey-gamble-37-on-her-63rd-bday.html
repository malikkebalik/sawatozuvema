Kris Jenner defies age in rare hugging photo with boyfriend Corey Gamble, 37, on her 63rd bday

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, September 22, 2024

Kris Jenner, the momager of the Kardashian/Jenner clan, took to Instagram to share some photos next to her boyfriend, Corey Gamble, on her 63rd birthday. She looked very young.

In one of the posts, Kris appeared wearing a long-sleeved, tight, black dress that had a V-cut neckline that made her look beautiful. Corey was right behind her wearing blue jeans and a green shirt showing some love.

Behind the couple, several black, white, and silver balloons adorned the room, followed by some bigger ones that read “KRIS.” For the caption, the 63-year-old momager thanked Corey for the “birthday love and surprises.”

A VERY HAPPY BIRTHDAY

After that, Kris uploaded a second image showing her sitting alone on a white couch while crossing her legs, showcasing her black boots. The balloon decoration was a couple of steps behind her, making the post even better.

“To top off the perfect day with the most amazing friends... thank you [Dee Ocleppo] [Thomas Hilfiger] for another birthday surprise!!! I LOVE and adore you!!!” wrote Kris in the caption.

HAVING FUN

The last post of her birthday showed the woman posing on a couch rocking her striking clothes and beautiful body. The aspect that surprised more people was how young the lady looked.

KIM AND KOURTNEY'S BIRTHDAY WISHES

Kim and Kourtney Kardashian also took to social media to send their best wishes to their mother. In Kim’s case, she uploaded a photo of Kris holding her daughter Chicago. The reality star revealed that her mother was her “rock” and that she was thankful for everything.

Kourtney, on the other hand, posted one showing only both of them posing on a yacht in beautiful outfits. “You are the definition of living your best life. I love you so much my mommy I don’t even have words to describe it. Thank you for being so fabulous,” wrote Kourtney.

HER RELATIONSHIP WITH COREY

Even though the relationship Kris has with her daughters is beautiful, there is another person who has been with her for a while now that brought joy to her life: Corey. As Cosmopolitan reported, they met each other around August 2014 during the birthday party of fashion designer Riccardo Tisci in Ibiza.

Soon after that, their relationship grew stronger, and they were spotted arriving and leaving places together. It wasn’t until April 2015 that Kris made it Instagram official by posting a photo of the couple in a double date with Billy Ray and Trish Cyrus. Little by little, their relationship has been getting better, and they enjoy each other’s company.
