Obituary: Raymond Chamberlin

It is with deep sadness that we announce the passing of Raymond Chamberlin, a beloved resident of Cleveland, Tennessee. Raymond was born in Newark Valley, New York, and lived a remarkable life until his peaceful departure on December 28, 2023, at the age of 92. His memory will forever be cherished by those who had the privilege of knowing him.

Personal Information

Raymond Chamberlin was a remarkable individual who touched the lives of many. Throughout his life, he exemplified resilience, kindness, and a strong sense of family values. Born in Newark Valley, New York, Raymond later made Cleveland, Tennessee, his home. He was a loving husband, devoted father, and doting grandfather. Raymond’s warm smile and gentle demeanor will be deeply missed by all who had the pleasure of knowing him.

Surviving Family Members

Raymond Chamberlin leaves behind a loving and supportive family who will forever cherish his memory. He is survived by his beloved wife, Janet Louise Chamberlin, who stood by his side through thick and thin. Raymond’s children, Brian Chamberlin, Ramona Howe, Coleen Freese (Charlie), and Jeff Chamberlin (Paulette), were fortunate to have him as their guiding light. Additionally, his stepsons Kenneth and Michael Dodge were blessed to have Raymond as a father figure in their lives.

Raymond’s legacy extends to his grandchildren, who brought immense joy to his life. Chris Howe, Wendy Howe, Heather Howe, Jennifer Howe, Gene Freese, Nichole Freese, Jessica Stamper, Kara Matthew, Joshua Chamberlin, Brent Chamberlin, Cory Chamberlin, Lora Aguila, Hannah Chamberlin, and Cala Bullington will forever carry his love and wisdom in their hearts.

During this difficult time, the Chamberlin family finds solace in the outpouring of support and condolences from friends and well-wishers. Your kind words and gestures are deeply appreciated as they navigate through their grief.

Funeral Arrangements

The funeral arrangements for Raymond Chamberlin have been carefully planned to honor his life and provide an opportunity for loved ones to come together and pay their respects. Please find the details below.

Visitation

A visitation will be held at the Companion Funeral & Cremation Service – Cleveland on Friday, January 5, 2024, from 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM. This will be a time for family, friends, and acquaintances to gather and share their memories of Raymond. The address for the visitation is 2415 Georgetown Rd NW, Cleveland, TN 37311.

During the visitation, guests will have the opportunity to offer their condolences to the Chamberlin family and provide comfort during this difficult time. Your presence and support will mean a great deal to them as they navigate through their grief.

Funeral Service

The funeral service for Raymond Chamberlin will take place at the same location, Companion Funeral & Cremation Service – Cleveland, on Friday, January 5, 2024, from 6:00 PM to 7:00 PM. This service will be a solemn and heartfelt tribute to Raymond’s life, celebrating his accomplishments, and honoring the impact he had on those around him.

During the funeral service, family members and close friends will have the opportunity to share their memories and stories of Raymond, highlighting the profound influence he had on their lives. It will be a time of reflection, remembrance, and a celebration of a life well-lived.

The Chamberlin family extends their gratitude to all those who will be attending the funeral service. Your presence and support will provide them with strength and comfort as they bid farewell to their beloved Raymond.
