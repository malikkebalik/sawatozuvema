Fabiana Semprebom is a Brazilian model who was discovered while shopping at a local mall in São Paulo. Over the years, she has walked for various fashion houses and designers including Victoria’s Secret, Alessandro Del’Acqua, Hugo Boss, Tibi, Baby Phat, Chanel, Armani Privé, Valentino, Marchesa, Nina Ricci, Jason Wu, Bottega Veneta, Celine, Stella McCartney, Hussein Chalayan, Vivienne Westwood, Wunderkind, Lacoste, Alexander Wang, Dolce & Gabbana, Monique Lhuillier, Mara Mac, Dsquared2, Roberto Cavalli, Michel Klein, Versace, Victor Dzenk, Victorio & Lucchin, Animale, Trussardi, Guy Laroche, Tadashi Shoji, Gianfranco Ferre, and Emporio Armani. Fabiana has also appeared on the covers of magazines like Elle Greece and Elle Mexico.
