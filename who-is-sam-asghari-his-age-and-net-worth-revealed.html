Who is Sam Asghari? His Age and Net Worth Revealed

With a net worth estimated to be between $5 million to $10 million, Sam Asghari has achieved a great deal for himself and has likewise garnered a lot of attention from the public and media due to his relationship with Britney Spears. Peruse on to find out more about the celebrity husband.

How Old is Sam Asghari?

Born Wesam Mohammad Asghari, the Iranian-American actor is 53 years old. Sam Asghari was born to his parents Mike Asghari and Fatima Asghari on March 3, 1994, in Tehran, Iran which was where he grew up and spent some part of his formative years. At the age of 12, he migrated to America, alongside his family,  and settled in Los Angeles. He later became an American citizen in 2012.

Not much is known about his family background as he has not revealed a lot of details about his family. However, we know that he was raised in a two-parent household and his father worked as a tow truck driver in the US while his mother’s occupation remains unknown.

He is the fourth and youngest child of his parents with three elder sisters named Ellie, Maddy, and Faye, all of whom he shares a close bond. His birth sign is Pisces and he belongs to the Persian ethnic background.

Speaking on his educational background, Sam Asghari is accomplished as he had his primary and had his high school education at Westlake High School after which he went further to get enrolled at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln and later went on to study criminal justice at Pierce College.

Sam Asghari is a Model, Actor, and Fitness Trainer

Sam Asghari was only four years old when he had his eyes set on football and he has since remained a super football fan. This also had driven him to participate in football while he was in high school.

While in the University, Sam played football for Nebraska in Lincoln and also went on to play for Los Angeles Moorpark College but could not follow his football passion through to the end as he had to quit playing in 1994 due to personal reasons, which he is yet to reveal.

In high school, Sam began getting involved in theatre classes, acting in plays such as Hamlet and Macbeth. When he was 21 years old, his elder sister, Faye, introduced him to modeling and this move opened up more opportunities for him.

In 2010, he began his modeling career and had his first runway show in Palm Springs, California for designer Michael Costello. He has since modeled for various magazines such as Iron Man, GQ, Men’s Health, and many others.

As an actor, Sam Asghari made his film debut playing the role of Sam Asghari in the sci-fi comedy movie Star Trek spoof Unbelievable alongside Snoop Dogg. He has also played the role of a security guard named Russell in the TV drama NCIS: Naval Criminal Investigative Service, which was one of the roles that brought him fame in the movie industry.

In 2021, he moved ahead in his acting career and featured in Black Monday season 3 playing the role of Giancarlo, a super sexy model who works for Pfaffashions. The actor has since been featured in many other movies.

The celebrity husband is also a fitness trainer and has a fitness program – Asghari Fitness. He took up this profession when he overcame his unhealthy eating habits and achieved a great body shape by losing weight loss. His fitness program is, therefore, focused on helping people become healthy. He creates and plans diet charts for his clients and also goes ahead to offer personalized workout plans suitable for his clients.

His Net Worth is Estimated in Millions

The actor and fitness trainer is said to have amassed a whooping net worth estimated to be between $5 million to $10 million. The majority of his income is gotten from his acting career, brand endorsements, and also from his social media presence and fitness program.

Sam is More Famous as Britney Spears’ Husband

Sam Asghari also has a love life – besides his professional life. The celebrity husband is married to America’s songstress and actress, Britney Spears – often referred to as Princess of Pop. Spears was born on December 2, 1981, in McComb, Mississippi, United States of America, and was raised by her parents in Kentwood, Louisiana. She is a well-recognized singer, dancer, and actress.

Sam’s wife started her career as an actress at a very young age, landing acting roles both in stage production and television shows. As a singer, she first signed under Jive Records in 1997 and made her debut album titled “Baby One More Time” which was released in 1999 and also got to release another titled “Oops!…I Did It Again.” These two albums became top hits and made it to international No. 1 hits.

Britney Spears has sure made a name for herself as an actress and also a musician. She was recognized as the best-selling female artist of the first decade of the 21st century and was ranked sixth on the Forbes list of the 100 Most powerful and influential celebrities in the world.

Sam and Britney Spears Met on Set Of Her “Slumber Party” Music Video

The actor was once in a relationship with American actress Mayra Veronica. After they had stayed together for some time, they broke up in 2016 and went their separate ways. Moving on from his relationship with Mayra Veronica, Asghari met famous singer and actress Britney Spears in October 2016 on the set of her “Slumber Party” music video.

According to Sam Asghari, it was love at first sight and they just clicked and exchanged phone numbers after talking to each other for the first time. The pair got acquainted with each other and began dating after which they took their relationship to the next level by getting engaged in September 2021.

Before getting hitched, Asghari and Spears signed a prenuptial agreement which was to protect Spear’s fortune worth $60 million. The lovebirds got married on June 9th, 2022, and their wedding was a private ceremony attended by close family and friends that took place at Britney Spears’s home located in California. Afterward, they moved to Spears’s new $11.8 million mansion located in Calabasas.

Sam Asghari’s wedding to Britney Spears was his first wedding but the third for Spears as she was once married to Jason Alexander in 2004 in Las Vegas for just 55 hours. That same year, she got married to Kevin Federline and had two boys – Sean Preston and Jayden James – in the marriage.

On April 11, 2022, Britney Spears had taken to her social media account to announce that she was expecting a child, which by the way was the first for Asghari, but unfortunately, it was never to be as she had a miscarriage the following month.

His Wife, Britney, Is 12 Years Older Than Him

Age they say is just a number particularly when two lovers are involved. This adage has proven to be true in the case of Sam Asghari and his wife, Britney Spears.

The pair have a huge age difference between them as Spears is 12 years older than Asghari, however, they have stayed happily in love for years now and this has not in any way affected their relationship.
