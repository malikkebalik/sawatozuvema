Kian Robert Lawley – A Brief Biography

Kian Lawley, an actor and internet personality, was born on 2 September 1995, in Sioux City, Iowa USA. He gained fame through his YouTube channels, superkian13 and kianlawley. Lawley has also ventured into acting, earning roles in films such as “Shovel Buddies” and “The Chosen”. These endeavors have significantly contributed to his current net worth.

His net worth was estimated to be $2 million in mid-2016, primarily amassed from his success in acting and on YouTube. His collaborations with various YouTubers and numerous awards have solidified his position in the industry. Lawley is one of the few YouTubers who successfully transitioned to an acting career, with his wealth expected to grow as he continues to pursue his career.

Kian Lawley’s Net Worth: $2 million

Lawley launched his career by creating the YouTube channels, kianlawley and superkian13, where he posted videos that gained popularity. He later became part of the YouTube group called Our2ndLife, before finding success in the film industry with “The Chosen” and “Shovel Buddies”. Lawley has also maintained a strong social media presence, particularly on Twitter and Instagram, with millions of followers on both platforms. Despite his social media commitments, Lawley has managed to thrive in the acting world, navigating seamlessly from online content creation to the big screen.

Regarding his personal life, Lawley was previously in a relationship with YouTuber Andrea Russett, though they separated in mid-2014, and he is reportedly single at present.
