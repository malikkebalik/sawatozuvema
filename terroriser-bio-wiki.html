Who is Terroriser?

Terroriser is an Irish YouTube content creator who creates auto-tuned gaming comedy videos.

In other languages, he is an online gamer who makes video content through his gameplay and is also a streamer.

He is famous for impersonating the Marvel and DC Comics villains in his videos.

Terroriser: Age, Parents, Siblings

Brian Michael Hanby, better known as Terroriser, was born on March 23, 1991, in Dublin, Ireland. He is 31 years old as of 2023.

He is a single child of his parents, but there is no information regarding his parents. He loves to have a low-key profile.

Brian holds Irish nationality and believes in Christianity religion. Similarly, his zodiac sign is Aries, and his lucky number is 7.

He started working a part-time job to support his parents financially at a very young age.

Terroriser: School, Education

Brian attended a traditional school in Dublin for primary education. But his grades were inferior as he was not a brilliant kid.

Terroriser was enrolled in college to get a diploma but dropped out to support his parents financially.

Terroriser: Professional Life, Career

Terroriser began his career as a YouTube content creator in 2011. He joined YouTube on May 9, 2011, named his channel Terroriser and posted a video titled ‘Crazy Black Man.’

As he started paying attention and developed his content, his channel grew and began to gain fans. Unexpectedly his channel started receiving significant subscriptions while he was still in college.

Slowly, he started receiving more than thousands of dollars daily through subscriptions. So, he quit working part-time and dropped out of college to focus on YouTube.

Terroriser started playing different online games so he could create content. Brian began creating content on Minecraft, Grand Theft Auto 5, UNO, Call Of Duty, Fortnite, Shot Gun Farmers, Mario Cart 8, VR Games, Q&A sessions, vlogs, etc., on his channel.

“Opening Christmas Gifts From Vanoss! (A Terroriser Christmas)”, ” I Smashed Another Controller…. Rest in peace”, “Vlog 9 Lui Caliber Squeaker Squad IRL Face Reveal”, and “Arnold Schwarzenegger Plays Black Ops” are some of the most popular and most viewed videos on his channel.

To this date, Terroriser has more than 3.85 million subscribers and has received more than 794k views on his channel.

Awards, Achievements
