Early Life and Family

Vicki Sue Hinds was born in Fort Carson, Colorado, and spent her formative years in Claflin, Kansas. She was raised in a loving family, with parents Doral Julian and Melva Julian (Harrod). Although her parents and siblings, Allen Ray Julian, Kenneth Harrod, and Anita Volker, passed away before her birth, their memory lived on in the hearts of those who knew them.

Vicki Sue Hinds was fortunate to have a supportive and caring family. She was joined in life’s journey by her spouse, Russell Hinds, who stood by her side through thick and thin. She also shared a special bond with her siblings: Tim Julian (Donna) of Dighton, Tony Julian of Stafford, Gary Julian (Amy) of Stafford, Andrew Julian (Jennifer) of Geneseo, and Pamela Larson of Spearfish. Additionally, her close friend Ramona Schwartz of Ellinwood was a constant source of companionship and support.
