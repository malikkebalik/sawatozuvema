Isaak Presley is an American actor known for his superior acting skills in various TV shows like Stuck in the Middle, Fuller House, Wet Hot American Summer: First Day of Camp, and Nicky, Ricky, Dicky & Dawn. He is also a singer and released his first album titled The Magic of Christmas in 2015. He is quite popular on social media with more than 1 million followers on Instagram.
