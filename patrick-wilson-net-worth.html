Professional Career of Patrick Wilson

In Todd Field’s “Little Children,” in which he played Brad Adamson, Wilson made his acting debut in 2006. He starred as Michael Shephard in the Golden Globe Award-nominated film “Running With Scissors,” which was produced and directed by Brad Pitt.

Wilson co-starred with Rose Byrne in James Wan’s horror movie “Insidious” and portrayed Lynch, the movie’s main antagonist, in 2010’s The A-Team. Insidious: Chapter 2, the 2013 sequel to the first movie, saw him return.
