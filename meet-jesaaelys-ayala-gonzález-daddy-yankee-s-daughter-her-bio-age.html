Meet Jesaaelys Ayala Gonzlez, Daddy Yankee's Daughter: Her Bio &amp; Age

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, August 18, 2024

Jesaaelys Ayala González is a celebrity who is famous among people as a social media influencer. She has also made her name as a makeup artist. But, even if social media didn't exist, she would still be a celebrity because of her father, Daddy Yankee, an insanely popular rapper.

The famous social media personality Jesaaelys was born in San Juan, Puerto Rico, on June 18, 1996, which means she is a Gemini. Let's learn some interesting facts about this beauty influencer.

González Had To Go Through A Surgery To Lose Weight

Jesaaelys may have gained fame and success, but her life was far from easy. Obesity has been a huge issue in the world for a while now, and unfortunately for her, she suffered from it.

The situation was so bad that González had no option but to get help to lose some weight. She had to go through gastric sleeve surgery, and she has no regrets about doing it. She weighs 143 lbs today, which can be credited to her brave decision to change her lifestyle choices.

Jessaleys, before surgery, weighed 227 lbs. She has suffered from being overweight for a very long time. Daddy Yankee's daughter weighed more than 220 lbs (100 kg) when she was just fourteen.

What Is Jesaaelys Relationship Status? 

The celebrity daughter Jesaaelys Ayala González is indeed in a relationship. She has dated Carlos Olmo for many years now. And, he is now more than just a boyfriend to her as the pair have already engaged.

This promising couple, Jesaaelys & Carlos, got engaged in the June of 2021. However, they haven't provided any info on their wedding, but it could happen anytime. She is regularly posting about her photographer boyfriend on her Instagram. 

Jesaaelys started seeing Carlos on November 17, 2017.  He is also a Puerto Rican like her. Her boyfriend is a huge baseball fan, as evident from his Insta. 

Education Background

The 5 Feet 8 Inches tall Jesaaelys has a successful career today through the help of social media, so she probably doesn't need to be too worried about her education. But that doesn't mean she never bothered to study. So, where did she go for education?

Following the footpath of his father, Jeremy has already produced a lot of music and also has performed live. It seems like everything is going well for him, as he has gained some fans doing this. 

Net Worth & Earnings Of Jesaaelys

Jesaaelys Ayala González is not just living off her father's name and is a celebrity in her own right. The woman has made a living through the internet, and her net worth is estimated to be $1 Million. Her net worth includes her income from social media, her beauty page & brand endorsement.

Her Father Daddy Yankee's Net Worth

Jesaaelys's Career

In addition, she has tried to put her hands on every social media as possible. And one of them is the Youtube platform where she is active on a channel @Jesaaelys. There she has amassed 35.3k followers.

Physical Appearance

About Jesaaelys Father Daddy Yankee

Daddy is one of the most prominent Latino music artists ever. The famous rapper managed to gain global success while not rapping in English, which is hard to do. And he did it at a time when Latin music wasn't mainstream popular, making his achievement even more impressive.

He has given many hit songs throughout his career, such as Gasolina, La Santa, X ÚLTIMA VEZ, and Dura. Despacito is his biggest hit, setting a record for the highest views on YouTube. Marysabel Huston-Crespo from CNN has mentioned what he said to his fans while announcing it. He said:

This race, which has been a marathon, finally sees the finish line. Now, I am going to enjoy what all of you have given me. People say that I made this genre worldwide, but it was you who gave me the key to open the doors to make it the biggest in the world.
