Lana Cordor Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 31, 2024

Who is Lana Condor?

Lana Condor is a Vietnamese-American actress who is immensely popular for her debut role of Jubilation Lee / Jubilee in the 2016 superhero film ‘X-Men: Apocalypse’.

Currently, she is working on a science fiction film ‘Alita: Battle Angel’ whereby, she is portraying the role of Koyomi.

What are the birth facts of Lana Cordor?

She was born on May 11, 1997, in Vietnam. Her nationality is Vietnamese-American and her ethnicity is Vietnamese.

Lana Condor was adopted by Mary and Robert ‘Bob’ Condor, a Yahoo! Sports executive editor, in Can Thơ, Vietnam.

Lana Cordor was raised by her parents along with her non-biological brother Arthur in Chicago, Illinois, United States of America.

Lana Condor: Education History

Talking about the educational background of Lana Condor, she attended the training center of Joffrey Ballet, the Gelsey Kirkland Academy of Classical Ballet, and the Alvin Ailey American Dance Theater when she was a child.

Later, Lana Cordor also joined New York Film Academy and Yale Summer Conservatory for Actors.

Professional Performing Arts School in New York City and  Notre Dame Academy were also her educational institution where she learned the most important lesson of her life.

She also joined  Loyola Marymount University in 2014.

 Lana Condor: Professional Life and Career

Lana Condor started her career in the year 2016 when she got the opportunity to portray the role of the mutant Jubilation Lee / Jubilee in Bryan Singer’s superhero film ‘X-Men: Apocalypse’.

After she worked for ‘X-Men: Apocalypse’, she also played the role of Li in the 2016 film ‘Patriots Day. In 2017, she is about to appear as Allison in the film ‘High School Lover’. Currently, she is working on her 2018 film ‘Alita: Battle Angel’ whereby, she has the role of ‘Koyomi’.
